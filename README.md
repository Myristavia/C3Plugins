# 🎮 Crystal Velocity - The Ultimate 2D Platformer

**The most addictive 2D platformer ever created!** Better than Super Mario World, faster than Sonic, and more engaging than any platformer you've ever played.

## 🌟 Features

### 🏃‍♂️ Advanced Movement System
- **Double Jump**: Master the skies with precise double jumping mechanics
- **Wall Jump**: Scale any surface with fluid wall jumping
- **Dash**: Lightning-fast dash ability with visual effects and cooldown management
- **Wall Slide**: Graceful wall sliding with speed control
- **Trail Effects**: Beautiful particle trails that follow your every move

### 🤖 Intelligent Enemy AI
- **6 Unique Enemy Types**:
  - 🚶 **Walker**: Basic patrol enemies
  - 🦘 **Jumper**: Aggressive jumping enemies
  - 🦅 **Flyer**: Aerial enemies with dive attacks
  - ⚡ **Spiker**: Charging enemies with deadly spikes
  - 🔫 **Shooter**: Ranged projectile enemies
  - 🛡️ **Heavy**: Armored enemies with ground pound attacks

### 🎯 Addictive Progression System
- **17 Unique Achievements** with XP rewards
- **Experience & Leveling System** with skill point rewards
- **Combo System** with up to 5x score multipliers
- **Daily Challenges** that refresh every day
- **Personal Best & Leaderboard** tracking
- **Unlockable Abilities** as you level up

### 💎 Collectibles & Power-ups
- **Crystals**: Shimmering collectibles with sparkle effects
- **Power-ups**: 5 different types with unique visual effects
  - ⚡ Speed Boost
  - 🦘 Enhanced Jump
  - 💨 Improved Dash
  - 🛡️ Shield Protection
  - 💰 Double Score

### 🎨 Visual Polish
- **Particle Systems**: Explosions, trails, and ambient effects
- **Screen Shake**: Impact feedback for satisfying gameplay
- **Parallax Backgrounds**: Multi-layered scrolling backgrounds
- **Smooth Animations**: Fluid character and enemy animations
- **Glow Effects**: Power-ups and abilities have beautiful glow effects

### 🏗️ Dynamic Platforms
- **8 Platform Types**:
  - Static, Moving, Vertical Moving
  - Jump Pads, Breakable Blocks
  - Rotating, Disappearing Platforms
  - Ground Platforms

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Download**: Clone or download this repository
2. **Open**: Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
3. **Play**: Click "START GAME" and begin your adventure!

```bash
git clone [repository-url]
cd crystal-velocity
open index.html
```

## 🏆 Achievements

Unlock 17 unique achievements by mastering different aspects of the game:

- 🦘 **Getting Airborne**: Perform your first jump
- 🌤️ **Sky Walker**: Perform 100 double jumps
- 🧗 **Wall Crawler**: Perform 50 wall jumps
- ⚡ **Speed Demon**: Use dash 200 times
- ⚔️ **First Blood**: Defeat your first enemy
- 🔥 **Combo King**: Achieve a 20x combo
- 💎 **Crystal Collector**: Collect 500 crystals
- 🏆 **Completionist**: Collect all crystals in a level
- 🏃 **Speed Runner**: Complete a level in under 60 seconds
- 👑 **Score Master**: Reach 100,000 points

## 📊 Combo System

Build massive combos by chaining actions:
- Collect crystals and defeat enemies to build your combo
- Higher combos = higher score multipliers (up to 5x)
- Combos break if you take damage or wait too long
- Visual feedback shows your current combo level

## 🎯 Daily Challenges

Fresh challenges every day keep the game exciting:
- **Crystal Rush**: Collect target number of crystals
- **Enemy Hunt**: Defeat specific number of enemies
- **Combo Challenge**: Achieve high combo multipliers
- **Speed Challenge**: Complete levels under par time
- **Perfect Play**: Complete levels without dying
